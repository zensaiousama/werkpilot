## Role Overview

We are looking for an experienced **French Proofreader** to join our freelance team. You will be responsible for ensuring all French-language content meets our high quality standards before delivery to clients.

## Responsibilities

- Proofread and edit French content for grammar, spelling, punctuation, and style
- Ensure translations from German/English to French are natural and idiomatic
- Maintain consistency with client-specific style guides and glossaries
- Flag cultural nuances or localization issues
- Collaborate with the content team on quality standards
- Meet tight deadlines without compromising quality
- Provide feedback on recurring language issues to improve upstream processes

## Requirements

- Native-level French proficiency (CH-FR, FR-FR, or BE-FR)
- Minimum 3 years of professional proofreading/editing experience
- Strong understanding of French grammar rules and regional variations
- Experience with business/marketing content preferred
- Familiarity with localization best practices
- Excellent attention to detail
- Ability to work independently and manage multiple projects
- Reliable internet connection and availability during CET business hours
