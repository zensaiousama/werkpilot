## Role Overview

We are looking for an experienced **Italian Proofreader** to join our freelance team. You will ensure all Italian-language content meets our rigorous quality standards, supporting our Swiss and Italian market clients.

## Responsibilities

- Proofread and edit Italian content for grammar, orthography, punctuation, and style
- Review translations from German/English to Italian for naturalness and accuracy
- Ensure content is culturally appropriate for the target audience (CH-IT or IT market)
- Maintain client-specific terminology and style guide compliance
- Flag localization issues and suggest improvements
- Provide quality feedback to content creation pipeline
- Meet project deadlines consistently

## Requirements

- Native-level Italian proficiency (CH-IT or IT-IT)
- Minimum 3 years of professional proofreading/editing experience
- Comprehensive knowledge of Italian grammar and style conventions
- Experience with business, marketing, or technical content
- Strong attention to detail and quality orientation
- Ability to handle multiple projects simultaneously
- Available during CET business hours (flexible scheduling)
- Reliable internet connection

## Nice to Have

- Experience reviewing AI-generated or machine-translated content
- Familiarity with CAT tools (SDL Trados, MemoQ, Memsource)

- Experience with SEO-optimized content
