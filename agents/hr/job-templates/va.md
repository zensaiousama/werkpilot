## About Werkpilot

Werkpilot is a Swiss AI automation startup that helps businesses streamline their operations through intelligent automation. We are building the future of work by combining human expertise with AI capabilities.

## Role Overview

We are seeking a resourceful, tech-savvy **Virtual Assistant** to support our growing operations. You will work closely with the founding team, handling a variety of administrative, organizational, and operational tasks that keep Werkpilot running smoothly.

## Responsibilities

### Administrative Support
- Email management, scheduling, and calendar coordination
- Document preparation and formatting
- Data entry and database management (Airtable, Google Sheets)
- Meeting preparation and minute-taking

### Operations Support
- Client communication and follow-up
- Invoice tracking and basic bookkeeping assistance
- Project coordination and deadline monitoring
- Research tasks and information gathering

### Digital & AI Tasks
- Managing and organizing digital files and workflows
- Basic content formatting and publishing
- Assisting with AI tool configuration and testing
- CRM data maintenance and updates

### Communication
- Draft professional emails and correspondence
- Coordinate between team members and freelancers
- Handle routine client inquiries
- Translate simple communications (DE/EN advantage)

## Requirements

- Minimum 2 years of virtual assistant or administrative experience
- Excellent written and verbal English communication
- Proficiency with Google Workspace (Docs, Sheets, Calendar, Drive)
- Experience with project management tools (Asana, Trello, Notion, or similar)
- Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
- Ability to prioritize tasks and manage time independently
- Reliable internet connection (minimum 20 Mbps)
- Available for at least 20 hours per week during CET-overlapping hours
- Comfortable working in a fast-paced startup environment

## Nice to Have

- German language skills (any level)
- Experience with Airtable, Slack, or Zapier
- Familiarity with AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)
- Basic understanding of bookkeeping/invoicing
- Experience supporting tech startups or founders
- Knowledge of Swiss business culture and practices
- Experience with CRM systems
